Download "How Endgame Looks"

 

To download the mod from my website and install it onto your computer, you'll have to follow the instructions.

Step 1. Download the mod

To download the mod to your computer, right click here (on the blue text) and click "Save Target as..." .

Pick a folder and a name that you want to save the file to, and then click Save.

Hold the Windows key on your keyboard, and then press the R key. You will see the same thing as in the screenshot.

In the textbox on the right of "Open:", type "%appdata%\.minecraft\mods" (without the quotes), then press Enter or click OK.

A new window of your file explorer will open in that folder.

If you get an error saying "Windows cannot find C:\Users\Bright_citadel\AppData\Roaming\.minecraft\mods" or something similar, then click here to fix the issue.

Once you have done the steps above and fixed the issue (if applicable), you can continue.

Open a new window of your file explorer by right-clicking its icon on your taskbar and clicking "File Explorer".

Navigate to the folder where you downloaded the file from earlier (likely Downloads).

Right click on the file and click "Copy".

 

Now, go back to the mods folder in the other window of your file explorer.

Right click on an empty space and choose "Paste".

You can now start Minecraft Forge on Minecraft 1.6.4, and you will have the mod installed.
