Troubleshooting

 

This page will help you fix this error while trying to install the mod:

Click on OK on the error message. You should then see the Run window again.

Clear all the text on the right of "Open:".

On the right of "Open:", type "%appdata%/.minecraft" (without the quotes).

A new window of your file explorer should open in a folder containing subfolders and other files.

Right click on an empty space, and move your mouse cursor to "New". Leave it there for a few seconds until a new menu opens.

Move your mouse to the right, and click on "Folder".

You should then see a new folder appear named "New Folder".

Right click on it, and click on "Rename".

Type "mods" (without the quotes) and press Enter or click on an empty space.

Now, open the "mods" folder you have just created.

Continue the installation guide.
